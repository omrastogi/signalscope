The Dealer Agreements are substantially identical in all material respects except as to the parties thereto. A national bank acts as the issuing and paying agent under the Program pursuant to the terms of an issuing and paying agent agreement. Under the Program, the Company may issue Notes from time to time, and the proceeds of the Notes will be used for general corporate purposes. The Dealer Agreements provide the terms under which the Dealers will either purchase from the Company or arrange for the sale by the Company of Notes pursuant to an exemption from federal and state securities laws.
