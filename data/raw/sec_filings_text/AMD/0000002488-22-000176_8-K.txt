On November 16, 2022, Mr. John Caldwell, Lead Independent Director and Chair of the Nominating and Corporate Governance Committee of the Board of Directors (the “Board”) of Advanced Micro Devices, Inc. (the “Company”), announced his retirement from the Board effective as of the same day. Mr. Caldwell’s decision to retire is not because of any disagreement with the Company. Ms. Denzel has served on the Board since 2014. Item 7.01 Regulation FD Disclosure.
