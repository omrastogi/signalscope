AMENDMENTS TO ARTICLES OF INCORPORATION OR BYLAWS; CHANGE IN FISCAL YEAR. On May 27, 2020, the shareholders of Amazon.com, Inc. (the “Company”) approved an amendment to the Company’s Restated Certificate of Incorporation (the “Certificate”) to lower the stock ownership threshold from 30% to 25% for shareholders to request that the Company call a special meeting of shareholders. The Board of Directors of the Company approved a corresponding amendment to Section 2.2.2 of the Company’s Amended and Restated Bylaws (the “Bylaws”), effective May 28, 2020. This description of the amendments to the Certificate and the Bylaws is not complete and is qualified in its entirety by reference to the text of the Certificate and the Bylaws, copies of which are filed as Exhibit 3.1 and Exhibit 3.2, respectively, to this Form 8-K.
